Finance recruiting

Job Summary Dongjin is an international company that is establishing a new factory located on the outskirts of Skellefteå. They have an agreement with Northvolt to supply the Carbon Nanotube Slurry (CNT) needed in battery manufacturing. Dongjin is considered a world leader in manufacturing the slurry, which is a raw material for batteries. Dongjin is now in need of recruiting two financial accountants to their operations in Skellefteå. The work as a financial accountant entails monthly, quarterly, half-yearly and annual financial. Furthermore, you handle salary reports, invoices, operating costs and communication with the accountants at the head office. It is an advantage if you have a university degree in economics and experience from work with similar tasks. You are used to handling different tasks at the same time and are good at planning and structuring your work. Administrative work seems natural to you. Language skills in English are a requirement, Swedish is meritorious. If you have experience in accounting, it is advantageous.

Operators

We are looking shift work to our production team. We would like to see that you are a process operator or similar.

You will play an important role in contributing to a safe workplace, which is our absolute highest priority.

Your duties will include:

* Check machines and materials used in production

* Perform risk analyzes according to routines

* Work proactively with the maintenance team

* Responsible for ensuring that errors and deviations that affect production are resolved and reported and analyzed

* Ensure that safety procedures such as Lock Out Tag Out during repair and inspection are complied with

* Participate in job rotation to develop the skills within your shift

* Work with continuous improvements
