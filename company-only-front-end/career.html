Finance recruiting

Job Summary Dongjin is an international company that is establishing a new factory located on the outskirts of Skellefteå. They have an agreement with Northvolt to supply the Carbon Nanotube Slurry (CNT) needed in battery manufacturing. Dongjin is considered a world leader in manufacturing the slurry, which is a raw material for batteries. Dongjin is now in need of recruiting two financial accountants to their operations in skellefteå. The work as an financial accountant entails monthly, quarterly, half-yearly and annual financial. Furthermore, you handle salary reports, invoices, operating costs and communication with the accountants at the head office. It is an advantage if you have a university degree in economics and experience from work with similar tasks. You are used to handling many different tasks at the same time and are good at planning and structuring your work. Administrative work seems natural to you. Language skills in English are a requirement, Swedish is meritorious. If you have experience in accounting, it is advantageous.

Email: career@dongjin.se
